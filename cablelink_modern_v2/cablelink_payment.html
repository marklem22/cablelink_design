Simple Process

How to Pay in 4 Easy Steps

Paying your Cable Link bill is fast and straightforward — pick your channel and follow these steps.

1

Choose a Channel

Pick from online banking, e-wallet, walk-in office, or accredited payment center — whichever is most convenient for you.

2

Enter Your Account Number

Your Cable Link account number is printed on your monthly statement of account (SOA). It's required for all payment channels.

3

Enter Amount & Confirm

Enter the exact amount due or any partial amount. Double-check the details, then confirm the transaction to proceed.

4

Keep Your Receipt

Save your confirmation receipt — digital or printed. Payment reflects in your Cable Link account within 24 hours on business days.
